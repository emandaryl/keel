# Start here

You don't need to know how to code. Here's all you do:

1. Open this Keel folder with an AI coding assistant — Claude Code, Cursor, or
   Antigravity. Any one works.
2. Copy the prompt below and send it to the assistant.
3. When it asks, describe your idea in plain words. Then just follow along.

That's it. Keel takes care of the rest.

---

## The prompt to paste

> Read AGENTS.md and follow it exactly. I'm not a developer, so don't ask me
> anything technical. Ask me what I want to build, confirm the plan with me in
> plain language, then build it, check that it works, and tell me how to open it.
